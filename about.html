About Us

We are a non-for-profit organization that builds the capacity of communities to ensure empowerment for various activities. Our sustainable and holistic interventions include providing free treatment and operations for disability patients like cerebella paralysis (C.P.) and polio, rehabilitation and physiotherapy, free food, medications, and shelter for those in need. We also provide services for orphans and semi-orphans, health, livelihood, and offer innovative solutions to deep-rooted development problems.

We enable individuals and organizations to raise and donate funds conveniently to any cause they care about, with offerings including crowdfunding, corporate giving, cause marketing, and philanthropy consulting.

Our Mission

To save lives, enable social protection, and defeat poverty. To drive social change by fostering an environment where young adults and children learn, lead, and thrive.

Our Vision

Our vision is to alleviate poverty by enabling the world to give,to help build a more influential, equal, and socially conscious society.
